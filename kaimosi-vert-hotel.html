Our Story

Where Luxury Meets
the Heart of Nature

Nestled near the ancient Kaimosi Forest in Shamakhokho, Vihiga County, Kaimosi Vert Hotel is Western Kenya's finest nature retreat. We offer an authentic escape where the call of the forest blends seamlessly with the warmth of genuine African hospitality.

Every room, every meal, and every interaction is crafted to leave you refreshed, restored, and deeply at peace. Whether you're seeking a romantic escape, a business retreat, or a family adventure — this is your sanctuary.
